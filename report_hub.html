Automated refresh schedule

  • Daily refresh (pulling in recently completed games) runs at 10pm PST every day
  • A master schedule refresh runs bi-weekly (Sundays, 5pm PST + Thursdays, 8am PST)
  • A weekly email reminder / updated hyperlink is sent out every Tuesday night at 9pm PST

Automation uses fixed UTC schedules; during daylight saving (PDT), Pacific times are typically about one hour later than these PST references.
